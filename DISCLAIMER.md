# Medical Disclaimer

## THIS SOFTWARE IS NOT A MEDICAL DEVICE

The Pi AI Clinic is a **decision-support tool** designed to assist community health workers and clinic staff in resource-limited settings. It is **not** a substitute for professional medical diagnosis, treatment, or advice.

### Important Limitations

1. **Not FDA/CE/WHO prequalified.** This software has not been reviewed or approved by any medical device regulatory authority.

2. **Clinical decisions require trained professionals.** All triage classifications, dosing recommendations, and treatment suggestions must be reviewed by a qualified health worker before acting on them.

3. **AI-generated text may contain errors.** While medical knowledge bases are sourced from WHO guidelines, the AI language model that generates natural-language explanations can produce inaccurate or misleading statements. Always verify against the structured classification shown on screen.

4. **Not for emergency use.** In a medical emergency, seek immediate professional medical care. Do not rely on this tool to manage acute emergencies.

5. **Dosing calculations are reference only.** All medication dosing must be verified by a qualified prescriber against current formulary guidelines before administration.

### Intended Use

This tool is intended for use by:
- Community health workers with basic training
- Clinic staff in settings where no physician is available
- Health worker training and education programs

It is intended to **augment**, not replace, the IMNCI (Integrated Management of Neonatal and Childhood Illness) chart booklet and other WHO clinical guidelines.
